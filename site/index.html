About us

Uh... Hello there! Welcome to &our shitposting site.

Circe Aurora is the name of core planet where everyone started existing. It was chosen after a vote between the citizens in town and a large city, as well as the headmates who lived remotely in a house.

The town, named Circe, is a mix of cultures you can find on earth. Having a section for Modern European, Semi Traditional/Modern Japanese, and Suburban USA. All connected together with public transport, roads, and beautiful lanes that are safe for pedestrians and cyclists. It is a safe place with consideration for nature and a modern lifestyle while remaining beautiful and true to their architectural styles. Plenty of plants line the sides of streets, parks with beautiful big trees, and plenty of places to drink water from. It is designed for the simplicities of life and to enjoy it while being connected instead of distant from nature.

The large city, Aurora, is a futuristic city from USA, but taking tips from other countries such as the Netherlands. Its large streets allow for effective public transportation through various means (IE. Buses, trams, metro, express monorail) or allows for safe passage for cyclists and pedestrians. The streets are lined with angled parking, trees and other plant life, bins, drinking fountains, benches, and with access to bathrooms and covered benches to get away from the sun and elements. It runs on fusion power and superconductors, meaning it doesn't need to pollute to stay powered on. Despite the population and street design, traffic doesn't jam and flows rather well, the drivers patient and driving smoothly. The city's skyline is filled with various tall skyscrapers, most over 100 floors high. These buildings are sometimes connected in the lower levels to acts as malls or other venues. But they also contain parking, residential, offices, and commercial rooms/sub-buildings to minimize travel needed. The buildings are also designed to combat against fires, floods, and other emergencies, allowing for easy and quick prevention of any minor disaster. The AIs and Robots help maintain the buildings alongside their humanoid counterparts, as robots and AI have full consciousness and rights.

This is one of many worlds our system is connected to. We might add more to it later.
